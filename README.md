# pee pee ass

pee pee ass launcher was made by "squillen" in 2018. it was a copy-pasted "gristlegun" from vanilla that fired 69 beach balls

This was the most important mod ever released imo

--- 
pee pee ass launcher 1: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1562652914

PPAL1 was originally a reupload of the mod after squillen privated the original.  
the reupload had a few changes (played random sounds & could fire a few random projectiles)  
then the original was made public again so PPAL1 was updated into a patch that added my changes onto the original

the original was later deleted and PPAL1 was left broken & unlisted for a few years  
PPAL1 is now an unmodified reupload of the original

---
pee pee ass launcher 2: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1666221023

PPAL2 was a second patch for the original mod that let it fire every vanilla projectile  
this too was left broken & unlisted for years after the original was deleted  

then after restoring PPAL1 i fixed & updated it  
the base weapon got the changes from the older versions of PPAL1 & the "fire any vanilla projectile" feature was moved into the upgraded weapon

---
pee pee ass launcher 3: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1915452201

after PPAL1 and PPAL2 were broken i made PPAL3  
it fires random projectiles, plays random sounds, places random images & flings you in random directions & rotates  
hundreds of images & sounds were provided by random discord users & i did not filter though them at all <3
